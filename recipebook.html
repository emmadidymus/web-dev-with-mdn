Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper.

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino cheese
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente.
  2. In a separate pan, cook the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs in a bowl, then mix in the cheeses and pepper.
  4. Drain the pasta and return to the pot. Quickly mix in the pancetta and egg mixture.
  5. Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper on top.

Chicken Curry

A flavorful and spicy chicken curry made with a blend of spices and coconut milk.

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan and saut√© the onion and garlic until soft.
  2. Add the curry powder and cook for another minute.
  3. Add the chicken and cook until browned on all sides.
  4. Pour in the coconut milk, bring to a simmer, and cook for 20 minutes until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, and serve with rice.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious homemade chocolate chip cookies with a crispy edge and chewy center.

Ingredients:

  • 200g all-purpose flour
  • 100g unsalted butter, softened
  • 150g brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 100g chocolate chips
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180¬∞C (350¬∞F) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla extract, and mix well.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  7. Scoop spoonfuls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes until golden brown.
  8. Allow to cool on a wire rack before serving.

Caesar Salad

A fresh and crunchy Caesar salad with homemade dressing and croutons.

Ingredients:

  • 1 head of romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, shaved
  • 100g croutons
  • 2 anchovy fillets (optional)
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 150ml olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the garlic, anchovies, mustard, and lemon juice.
  2. Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while whisking to create an emulsion.
  3. Season the dressing with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Toss the chopped lettuce with the dressing until well coated.
  5. Add the croutons and shaved Parmesan cheese on top.
  6. Serve immediately.
